Plaid Link

1. Run python3 scripts/plaid_create_link_token.py and paste the link_token below.
2. Click Open Plaid Link, connect your bank (Chase OAuth will redirect to chase.com).
3. Copy the public_token from the Result box.
4. Run python3 scripts/plaid_exchange_public_token.py <public_token> to get the access_token.
5. Add PLAID_TOKEN_CHASE=access-production-... to .env.
